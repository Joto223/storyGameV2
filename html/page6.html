The element of surprise is a crucial element of any successful operation. Having this to his advantage, Vega decides to throw caution to the wind.

One forceful kick to the flimsy wooden door was all it took to send it flying open. A look of surprise crossed Users face giving Vega all the confidence he needed. Crossing the thresh hold and unsheathing the dagger strapped to his back in one fluid motion.

Users face, still a mask of fear and surprise turned to the young girl, who Vega noticed for the first time did no seem to be concerned in the unexpected appearance of a strange man I her doorway.

The first indication that Vega had that something was wrong was the searing pain originating from his feet. Looking down he noticed the faint etchings in the floor that indicated the presence of a magic seal.

The barest hint of a smirk was the last thing that Vega saw before the flames consumed him.
